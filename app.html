How It Works

This tool uses a 3-step process to find missing images:

  1. Get categorized products from your storefront catalog (smaller file, parsed first)
  2. Filter to online products only using online-flag from master catalog (global or site-specific)
  3. Check images for products that are both categorized AND online

Two types of image issues are identified:

  • Missing Image Group: Products without an <image-group> matching your view type
  • Missing Image File: Products with image groups but files not found on WebDAV
